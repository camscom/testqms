<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>

#### **Last updated:** March 14, 2026

<br /><br />

This Privacy Policy explains how **QMSTesting.com** (“we”, “our”, “the site”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to transparency and to respecting your privacy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### **Information you provide directly**
- Contact information (if you email us)
- Any information you voluntarily submit through forms or comments

### **Automatically collected information**
When you visit the site, we may collect:
- IP address (anonymized if analytics cookies are rejected)
- Browser type and device information
- Pages visited and time spent on the site
- Referring URLs

This information may be collected through cookies or similar technologies. You can control these through our cookie banner.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Operate and maintain the website  
- Improve site performance and content  
- Analyze traffic patterns  
- Provide personalized content (if you consent)  
- Respond to inquiries  

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information.

---

## 3. Cookies and Consent

We use a cookie banner powered by Silktide to give you control over:

- Necessary cookies  
- Analytics cookies  
- Advertising/personalization cookies  

You can change your preferences at any time using the cookie icon on the bottom-left corner of the site.

For full details, see our [Cookie Policy](/cookie-policy).

---

## 4. Third‑Party Services

We may use trusted third‑party tools such as:

- **Google Analytics** (analytics, if accepted)  
- **Google Ads / Personalization** (advertising, if accepted)  

These services may process data outside of Canada. We ensure they follow recognized privacy and security standards.

---

## 5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Visitors)

For visitors from the EU/UK, we process data based on:

- **Consent** (analytics, advertising)  
- **Legitimate interest** (site security, necessary cookies)  
- **Contract** (responding to inquiries)  

---

## 6. Your Rights

### **For Canadian Visitors (PIPEDA)**
You may:
- Request access to your personal information  
- Request correction  
- Withdraw consent  

### **For EU/UK Visitors (GDPR)**
You may:
- Access your data  
- Request deletion  
- Restrict processing  
- Object to processing  
- Withdraw consent  
- Request data portability  

To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

---

## 8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

---

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact **QMSTesting.com** at *contact@qmstesting.com*. 

---

## 10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “last updated” date will reflect the most recent changes.
